You will record 4 short videos:
• Two indoors
• Two outdoors

Each video must be 20 seconds maximum.

Video One: Indoors - Neutral Face

Hold still and look at the camera for 5 seconds, then slowly move your head up, then down, then turn it toward your left shoulder, and then toward your right shoulder.

Video Two: Indoors - Surprised Face

Make a surprised face (wide eyes, eyebrows up). Hold still and look at the camera for 5 seconds, then slowly move your head up, then down, then turn it toward your left shoulder, and then toward your right shoulder.

Video Three: Outdoors - Neutral Face

Repeat the same movements outdoors.

Video Four: Outdoors - Serious Frown

Repeat the same movements outdoors with a serious frown.
