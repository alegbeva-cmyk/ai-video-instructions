Welcome!

Hi, welcome to the program and thank you for joining the study.

You will complete 4 short videos:

  • 2 videos indoors
  • 2 videos outdoors

Important: Every single video must be 20 seconds maximum.

Video 1: Indoors - Neutral Face

Hold still and look at the camera for 5 seconds.

Then slowly:

  • Move your head up.
  • Move your head down.
  • Turn your head toward your left shoulder.
  • Turn your head toward your right shoulder.

Video 2: Indoors - Surprised Face

Make a surprised face:

  • Wide eyes
  • Eyebrows up

Hold still and look at the camera for 5 seconds.

Then slowly:

  • Move your head up.
  • Move your head down.
  • Turn your head toward your left shoulder.
  • Turn your head toward your right shoulder.

Video 3: Outdoors - Neutral Face

Hold still and look at the camera for 5 seconds.

Then slowly:

  • Move your head up.

  • Turn your head toward your left shoulder.
  • Turn your head toward your right shoulder.

Video 4: Outdoors - Serious Frown
